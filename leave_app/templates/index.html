SERVICES

LEAVE APP is an application that enables active employees to apply for leave to their Human Resource office for approval. This service is meant to improve the previously outdated and quite franckly time consuming paper work process and also speed up the application response time from the Human Resource's office. Basically efficiency is what separates this service from the current tidious alternative.

STEPS

  • Log in using provided login credentials.

  • Update employee profile page.

  • Enter next of kin data for emergency purposes.
  • Enter leave information.

  • Confirm information entered is authentic to employee's credentials.

  • Submit leave form.
  • Wait for application's approval from Human Resource's office.

  • If approved, have a nice leave. If not, read HR's reason and respond appropriately.
